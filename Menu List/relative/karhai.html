Karhai Gosht

Karhai Gosht, also known as Kadhai Gosht, is a popular dish in Pakistani. It is a flavorful and aromatic preparation of tender meat cooked in a karahi, a traditional thick-bottomed, circular cooking pot.

Karhai Gosht is typically made with mutton or lamb, although it can also be prepared with chicken or beef. The meat is cut into bite-sized pieces and then cooked with a combination of spices and ingredients that give the dish its distinct taste.
