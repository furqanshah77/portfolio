Biryani

Biryani is a popular and flavorful rice dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent. It is known for its aromatic blend of spices and the combination of basmati rice with meat, vegetables, or both. Biryani is a complete meal in itself and is often served with raita (yogurt-based condiment), salad, or pickles. It involves marinating meat in yogurt and spices, layering it with partially cooked rice, and slow-cooking it to perfection. Biryani is celebrated for its rich flavors, fragrant aroma, and the perfect balance of spices, making it a beloved dish for special occasions and gatherings.
