Reshmi Kabab

Reshmi Kabab, also known as Reshami Kabab, is a delicious and succulent kebab dish that originates from the Pakistan. "Reshmi" translates to "silk" in Urdu, referring to the smooth and silky texture of these kebabs. They are made with minced meat, typically chicken or sometimes lamb, combined with various aromatic spices and ingredients.
